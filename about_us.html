About Us

Anduru Insurance Company was incorporated in Kenya and licensed to transact General insurance business.
It was licensed and began full operations in the year 2012.
It has a team of highly qualified, experienced and dynamic staff who render personalized, efficient and professional insurance services.
Our operations are on a specifically tailored computer system organized to render the most efficient service to customers.
We pride ourselves on strong asset base and unique countrywide branch network ensuring top quality services to our clients.
